Welcome

NEUB Sports Club is the umbrella organization for all sport activities in NEUB. It has a long history and tradition in sports with current and ex-students at national level. We have reputation of performing above expectations and approach on playing hard to win while treating our opponents with much respect within the rules. Beside sports, NEUB Sports Club is committed to brush up the leadership and organizational capabilities as well as voluntary activities of the students.
